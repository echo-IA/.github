# Welcome to **echo-IA**

**echo-IA** (*Enabling Cosmology with Homogenized Observations of Intrinsic Alignments*)  
is a collaboration of researchers working on **intrinsic alignments (IA)** across modeling, observations, and simulations.  

We serve as a **central hub** that connects efforts, datasets, and tools from many teams, making IA research more **cohesive, comparable, and reproducible**.  

Through shared standards and open infrastructure, we aim to accelerate progress toward a unified understanding of intrinsic alignments across surveys and methods.

---

Visit our website: **[echoia.org](https://echoia.org)**
